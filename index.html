ABOUT US

Syracuse International Film Festival (SIFF) is dedicated to engaging and inspiring the CNY community through film and media while fostering spaces that champion diversity, equity, and belonging. We prioritize intersectional representation, curate stories that reflect a wide range of identities and experiences, and use our platform to expand accessibility, uplift historically excluded voices, and raise awareness of pressing social issues.
